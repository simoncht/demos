How it works

Four simple steps

From application to comfortable home — we make it easy.

1

Prequalify in about a minute

Fill out a short application. Checking your options does not impact your credit score — no hard pull just to see what you qualify for.

2

Choose your plan

Select the monthly payment and term that fit your budget. Terms range from 3 to 60 months with APRs from 0 to 35.9%.*
