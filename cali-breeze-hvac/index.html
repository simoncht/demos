FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Why is my air conditioner running but not cooling?
Usually it's low refrigerant, a dirty filter or coil, or a failing capacitor or compressor. We diagnose the actual cause before quoting — so you only pay to fix what's wrong.
How do I know if my HVAC system needs repair or replacement?
It comes down to age (10–15 yrs), the repair cost versus the system's value, and efficiency. We'll give you an honest recommendation — and we won't push a replacement you don't need.
How often should HVAC maintenance be performed?
Twice a year is ideal — AC in spring, heating in fall. Regular tune-ups prevent breakdowns, keep efficiency up, and extend the life of your system.
What areas does Cali Breeze serve?
Fallbrook and all of North San Diego County — Bonsall, Vista, Oceanside, San Marcos, Escondido, and nearby communities (including parts of South Riverside).
What's the most expensive AC part to repair?
The compressor (and the evaporator coil) are the priciest. If one fails on an older unit, we'll be straight with you about whether a repair or a full replacement is the smarter spend.
How much does it cost to fix an AC in California?
It ranges widely with the issue and the unit. We give a clear, upfront quote before any work — no surprises.
How can I lower my air-conditioning bill in California?
Routine maintenance, a smart thermostat, sealing leaky ducts, and a high-efficiency unit all help. We're happy to point out the cheapest wins first.
