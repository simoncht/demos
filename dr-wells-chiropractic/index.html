Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Do you take my insurance?
Most major insurance plans are welcome, and we'll help you understand exactly what your coverage includes before treatment begins. We also offer straightforward self-pay options.
Does an adjustment hurt?
For most people, no. Adjustments are gentle, and Dr. Wells tailors the technique to your comfort level — including low-force methods if you prefer. Many patients feel relief right away.
What should I bring to my first visit?
Just your ID and insurance card (if using insurance), and wear comfortable clothing. Plan for about 45–60 minutes so we can do a proper exam and build your plan.
How many visits will I need?
It depends on what's going on — some issues resolve in a few visits, others benefit from ongoing care. You'll always get an honest recommendation and never any pressure.
Can chiropractic help with my headaches or sciatica?
Often, yes. Tension headaches and sciatica frequently stem from spinal and nerve irritation that responds well to chiropractic care. We'll assess your specific case at your first visit.
